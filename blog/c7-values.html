Exploring My (Inner) Values

08/21/15

In this chart are the values that depict my happiest, my proudest, and the most satisfied times in my life.

EXPLORING YOUR VALUES
Spirituality Adventure Freedom
Introspection Growth Insight
Creativity Quality Pleasure
Coolness under fire Competence Challenge
Tranquility Effectiveness Community
Wisdom Integrity Will-power

In general I try very hard to live up to these values. There are certainly things that I’d like to be more involved with my community. I belong to several very dear and loving communities. They love me and embrace my uniqueness. I try to give back in ways that I excel such as building and designing things but I’d like to get more involved still. I’m an introvert and people take up a lot of my energy so I’ve always felt like I’m on the periphery of these communities.

I think the thing that I value most is Adventure. I have a very curious mind and without new adventures I think a lot of my other values would cease to exist. I’m a person that needs to explore new ideas, new concepts, new careers ( :P ). It keeps me growing as a person and challenged in life.

Advice? Ask me anything about building stuff or a design challenge. I’m the go to guy for anyone that I know. I’m good at figuring out how things are made, put together and I’m really good with my hands ☺
