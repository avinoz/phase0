Thoughts in my head

Who am I you ask? I’m just a guy who is curious about the world and likes building things. I’ve visited forty-three countries, lived in seven and have built buildings in six countries. Yes, I design buildings… and yes I build stuff in my shop, stuff like a 16’-0” tall pinwheel!

So why am I here doing DevBootCamp you ask? Well, I’d been dabbling on public art of late and I know how to maneuver around design, engineering and fabrication but the one thing that I’m clueless in is programming. It started off at that and then I got rather addicted to it which brought me here, making this website.

I'm stoked about exploring this new territory of programming! To see the stuff that my design biz does, click the tab Things I've Done. Or check out my resume if you don't dig linked-In.
